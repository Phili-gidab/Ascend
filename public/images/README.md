# Images

Drop real photos here and they appear automatically (the site shows branded
placeholders until then). Use **real, consented** photos of people and
communities — they are central to NGO trust.

Recommended: JPG/WebP, ~1600px on the long edge, optimised (<300 KB each).

Expected files (referenced in `src/data/site.js`):

| File                      | Where it appears        | Suggested subject                          |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| `hero.jpg`                | Hero                    | Warm, hopeful photo of person(s) you serve |
| `program-health.jpg`      | Programs → Health       | Health / WASH / MHPSS activity             |
| `program-education.jpg`   | Programs → Education     | Inclusive, accessible classroom            |
| `program-protection.jpg`  | Programs → Protection    | Safe space / counseling / safeguarding     |
| `program-economic.jpg`    | Programs → Economic      | Livelihood / vocational training           |
| `story-1.jpg` … `story-3.jpg` | Impact stories      | Portraits for testimonials                 |

> **Consent & dignity:** only publish photos with informed consent. Avoid
> "poverty-porn"; show people with agency and dignity.
